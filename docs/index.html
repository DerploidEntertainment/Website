What is Derploid?

Part Dan. Part Matt. All Derp.

Matt and Dan have been making games since 2017. What started as a chance encounter has turned into a stimulating creative and commercial adventure. Derploid was founded on the belief that engaging, challenging gameplay experiences make the world a better place—that games can be fun and lucrative, without being exploitative.
